ABOUT US

Welcome to PATEÑO JEWELLERS, your #1 source of premium quality jewellery.

We're dedicated to giving you the very best of jewellery - engagement and wedding rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings - with a focus on QUALITY, AFFORDABILITY, DURABILITY, and CUSTOMER SERVICE.

Founded in 2022 by jewel experts, PATEÑO JEWELLERS has come a long way from its humble beginnings. When the team first started out, their passion for "excellent quality but affordable jewellery pieces" drove them to do tons of research so that they can offer you long-lasting jewellery without spending much.

We now serve customers all over the US, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into a REALITY.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
